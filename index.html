Hello~

We are a digital comic studio based in Indonesia. Our team has professional experience creating and developing comics in manga or webcomic/ webtoon format. We have a well-rounded skill set and experience in developing comic IP.

We are well-organized, well-managed, efficient, and attentive to detail. We take joy in crafting original & visually stunning comics that resonate with your unique story ideas and bring your artistic imagination to life.

Along our journey of creating comics, we believe that producing meaningful comics represents the next level beyond merely focusing on high quality one. The story itself is the world we aim to draw all audiences into, and our art serving as the key to captivate and immerse them.

WHAT OUR TEAM DO

Character Design

  • Visual Development
  • Illustration
  • Design

Designing a character based on your brief. Draw the full-body in front and back view with his/ her nature pose to show not only how they look visually but also to bring out their personal character.

A set of character’s faces & expressions in different angles will be drawn as well as wrapping up the visual development.

Script Writing

  • Story Development
  • Dialog
  • SFX

Converting your written story into a script and streamlines the comic production process by providing a clear plan for each page and panel. It helps maintain consistency in storytelling, art style, and characterization throughout the comic.

Manga

  • Black & white with tone
  • Inking

Sometimes our client came with a magnificent story based on his/ her experience, or purely from his/ her imagination and interest.

We breakdown the steps from designing characters ⎯ creating storyboard ⎯ detailing the sketch ⎯ cleaning-up the line art ⎯ end-up with inking process.

Silent Manga

  • No dialogue manga
  • Inking

The SILENT MANGA AUDITION® (SMA) is a manga audition exclusively for manga without dialogue.

In 2023 TEKADVERSE created this manga based on a client's request for SMA with a manga titled “You Who Pulled Me From My Loneliness”.

It explores the themes of Loneliness, the ability to change, accepting help, and finding a place where one feels welcomed

web-comic

A form of digital comics that can be accessed and read online through websites or platforms.

We have some webcomic project along 2021 to 2024 from Romance - Slice of Life to Fantasy - Sci-fi - Action.

3D Assets

  • 3D buildings, houses, cafe, etc
  • 3D props: vehicles & sets

3D assets is an important part for any comic format nowadays for easy reusable background. Besides, you’ll increase the efficiency, consistency, complexity, variety, dynamic camera angles, integration with the art styles, & workflow flexibility.

We can create custom assets to match the setting or environment of your story, it will enhance your comic visual and unique vibes.

Story-boarding

  • Translating the script
  • Panel layout, pacing, & t iming
  • Visualizing action, angle & shots

In our comic projects we always do the storyboard and share to the client whether the breaking down of the scenes and the key elements like characters, locations, actions, and emotions, also the framing, camera angles, and transitions between panels are seamless & fulfill the client expectations.

Video Story-boarding

  • Translating the Script
  • Frame, Pacing, & Timing
  • Visualizing Action, Angle & Shots

We also creating storyboard for ads video, short movie, even animation. We literally do the storyboard for 1 to 3 minutes short video in 30 to 90 frames. The client came with the characters brief and script for the video and we breakdown the frame-by-frame storyboard.
